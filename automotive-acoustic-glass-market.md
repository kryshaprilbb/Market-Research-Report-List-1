<p><ul><li>Double Deck Glass</li><li>Three Layers of Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive acoustic glass market features two primary types: double deck glass and three layers of glass. Double deck glass consists of two layers of glass with a sound-dampening interlayer, effectively reducing noise entering the vehicle. In contrast, three layers of glass incorporate an additional layer, enhancing sound insulation and providing superior noise reduction. Both types are designed to improve passenger comfort by minimizing external noise, enhancing the overall driving experience, and contributing to vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>

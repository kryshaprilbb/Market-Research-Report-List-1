Biological inks consist of living cells suspended in a medium for creating viable tissues. Organ-class materials are specialized biocompatible substances designed to replicate specific organ features. Other materials encompass a range of innovative substances used for diverse bioprinting applications, enhancing functionality and versatility.</p></p>

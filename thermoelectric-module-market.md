<p><p>The thermoelectric module market is primarily segmented into single stage and multistage modules. Single stage modules are designed for straightforward applications, providing efficient cooling or heating in compact spaces. In contrast, multistage modules use multiple thermoelectric junctions to achieve greater temperature differentials, making them suitable for more demanding applications requiring significant thermal management. Both types are critical in various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial processes, where precise temperature control is essential for performance and reliability.</p></p>

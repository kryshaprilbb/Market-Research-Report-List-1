<p><strong>The Inkjet Colorant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye Inkjet Colorant</li><li>Pigment Inkjet Colorant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet colorant market is primarily divided into two types: dye-based and pigment-based colorants. Dye inkjet colorants are water-soluble and deliver vibrant colors with smooth gradients, making them ideal for photos and images. However, they are less resistant to UV light and water. In contrast, pigment inkjet colorants use tiny solid particles that provide excellent durability and fade resistance, making them suitable for professional prints and archival applications, although they may offer a narrower color range and can be more expensive.</p></p>

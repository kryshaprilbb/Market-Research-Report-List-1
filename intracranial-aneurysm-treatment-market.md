<p><p>The intracranial aneurysm treatment market comprises several key types. Surgical clipping involves placing a clip around the base of the aneurysm to prevent blood flow and reduces the risk of rupture. Endovascular coiling employs tiny coils to fill the aneurysm and promote clot formation, minimizing the risk of hemorrhage. Flow diverters are stents placed across the neck of the aneurysm, redirecting blood flow to promote healing. Other treatments may include balloon-assisted techniques and pipeline embolization devices, expanding the care options for patients.</p></p>

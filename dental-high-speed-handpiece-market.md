<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust Handpiece</li><li>Outside Exhaust Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental high-speed handpiece market is primarily segmented into two types: inside exhaust handpieces and outside exhaust handpieces. Inside exhaust handpieces evacuate dental debris and coolant through internal channels, keeping the workspace cleaner and offering a more focused airflow. Conversely, outside exhaust handpieces expel air and debris externally, often resulting in a more significant respiratory mist in the work area. Both types serve the critical function of facilitating efficient dental procedures, with varied applications based on user preferences and clinical settings.</p></p>

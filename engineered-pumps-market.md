<p><p>The engineered pumps market features various types, including Francis turbine pumps, volute pumps, and vertical turbine pumps. Francis turbine pumps efficiently handle high-flow, low-head applications, utilizing a radial flow design. Volute pumps facilitate the transport of liquids by converting kinetic energy to pressure, ideally suited for various industrial uses. Vertical turbine pumps are designed for deep installations, drawing fluid from underground sources, making them essential for water supply and irrigation systems. Each type serves distinct operational requirements in diverse sectors.</p></p>

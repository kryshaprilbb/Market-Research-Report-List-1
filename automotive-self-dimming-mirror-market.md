<p><strong>The Automotive Self-dimming Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outer Self-dimming Mirror</li><li>Inside Self-dimming Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive self-dimming mirror market comprises two primary types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors are designed for vehicle exteriors, automatically adjusting to reduce glare from headlights of trailing vehicles, enhancing night-time driving safety. Inside self-dimming mirrors are located within the vehicle cabin and similarly minimize glare from incoming traffic, improving driver comfort and visibility. Both types utilize electrochromatic technology, promoting a safer and more pleasant driving experience by automatically managing light exposure.</p></p>

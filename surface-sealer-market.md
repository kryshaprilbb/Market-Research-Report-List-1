<p><p>The surface sealer market is segmented into three main types: penetrating sealers, film-forming sealers, and others. Penetrating sealers absorb into the substrate, providing protection against moisture and stains while allowing breathability. Film-forming sealers create a protective layer on the surface, offering a glossy finish and enhanced durability against wear and chemicals. The "others" category includes specialized sealers designed for specific applications or materials, catering to unique requirements such as UV protection or color enhancement, broadening the market's versatility.</p></p>

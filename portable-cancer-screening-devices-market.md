<p><p>The Portable Cancer Screening Devices market includes technologies like Infrared Thermography and Electrical Impedance Spectroscopy. Infrared Thermography utilizes thermal imaging to detect abnormal heat patterns associated with tumors, providing a non-invasive screening method. Electrical Impedance Spectroscopy involves measuring the electrical properties of tissues to identify cancerous changes, capitalizing on differences in cellular composition. Both devices enable early detection and monitoring of cancer, offering accessible and cost-effective screening options compared to traditional methods, thus enhancing patient care and outcomes.</p></p>

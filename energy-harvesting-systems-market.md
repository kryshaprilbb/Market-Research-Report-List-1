<p><p>Energy harvesting systems encompass various technologies that convert ambient energy into usable electrical power. Photovoltaic systems utilize solar radiation, converting sunlight directly into electricity. Thermoelectric generators exploit temperature gradients to generate power from heat. Piezoelectric devices harness mechanical stress from vibrations or movements, converting it into electrical energy. Electrodynamic systems generate energy through the motion of conductors in magnetic fields. Each of these market types addresses different energy sources and applications, catering to diverse needs for sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>

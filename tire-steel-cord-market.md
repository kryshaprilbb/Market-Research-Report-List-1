<p><p>The tire steel cord market is categorized into Normal Tensile (NT), High Tensile (HT), and Super Tensile (ST) types based on strength and durability. NT cords offer adequate performance for standard tires, balancing strength and cost. HT cords provide enhanced strength, improving tire performance and longevity, making them suitable for higher-performance vehicles. ST cords deliver superior tensile strength and flexibility, ideal for high-load and specialized applications, such as racing or heavy-duty tires, ensuring optimal safety and performance under extreme conditions.</p></p>

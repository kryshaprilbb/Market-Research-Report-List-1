<p><p>The cold chain warehousing market consists of various types designed to preserve perishable goods. Refrigerated containers maintain optimal temperatures during transport, ensuring product integrity. Blast freezers quickly freeze items to retain freshness and prevent ice crystal formation. Chillers are used for maintaining cooler temperatures for products that require refrigeration but not freezing. Cold rooms provide large, temperature-controlled storage areas, catering to diverse industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals, ensuring products remain safe and viable throughout the supply chain.</p></p>

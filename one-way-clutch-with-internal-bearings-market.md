<p><p>The one-way clutch market, particularly focusing on internal bearings, includes two primary types: ball bearing clutches and roller bearing clutches. Ball bearing clutches utilize small spheres to facilitate smooth engagement and disengagement, providing high efficiency and minimal friction. In contrast, roller bearing clutches employ cylindrical rollers, offering greater load capacity and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types ensure reliable torque transmission and are essential in various mechanical systems, enhancing performance in automotive, industrial, and aerospace sectors.</p></p>

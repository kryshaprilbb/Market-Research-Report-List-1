<p><p>Composite pipes in the oil and gas market are categorized into two main types: clad pipes and non-metal composite pipes. Clad pipes consist of a metal core enveloped by a corrosion-resistant composite layer, enhancing durability and resistance to harsh environments. Non-metal composite pipes, made from advanced materials like fiberglass or carbon fiber, offer lightweight, flexibility, and excellent resistance to chemicals and corrosion. Both types aim to improve efficiency, reduce maintenance costs, and ensure safe transport of hydrocarbons.</p></p>

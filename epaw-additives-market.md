<p><p>The EP/AW additives market is segmented into two primary types: Extreme Pressure (EP) additives and Anti-Wear (AW) additives. EP additives are designed to provide protection under high-load conditions by forming a protective film on surfaces, preventing damage during extreme pressure scenarios. AW additives, on the other hand, minimize friction and wear in less severe conditions, extending the lifespan of machinery and lubricants. Together, these additives enhance the performance and durability of industrial fluids and lubricants.</p></p>

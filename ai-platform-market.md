<p><p>AI platforms are categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise AI platforms are installed locally on a company's hardware, offering greater control over data and security but requiring significant infrastructure investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based AI platforms are hosted on remote servers, providing scalability, easier updates, and lower upfront costs. They enable organizations to leverage advanced AI capabilities quickly without the burden of managing physical infrastructure, making them a popular choice for many businesses.</p></p>

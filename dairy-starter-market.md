<p><p>The dairy starter market can be categorized into three main types: Mesophilic, Thermophilic, and Probiotic cultures. Mesophilic starters thrive at moderate temperatures, typically used in cheese and yogurt production. Thermophilic starters function at higher temperatures, ideal for products like strained yogurt and certain cheeses. Probiotic cultures, containing beneficial bacteria, promote gut health and are increasingly popular in functional foods, enhancing the nutritional profile of dairy products while catering to the rising consumer demand for health-oriented choices.</p></p>

<p><p>The automatic shut-off valves market is primarily categorized into hydraulic and electric types. Hydraulic type valves utilize fluid pressure to operate, providing reliable performance in high-pressure systems often used in industrial applications. In contrast, electric type valves employ electrical signals to open or close, enabling precise control and integration with automated systems, commonly found in residential and commercial settings. Both types offer safety and efficiency, playing crucial roles in fluid management across various industries.</p></p>

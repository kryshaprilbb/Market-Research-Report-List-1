<p><p>Rust Buster market types are categorized based on their pH levels. Acidity Rust Buster products have a low pH, effectively removing rust through acidic reactions, making them ideal for heavy corrosion. Alkaline Rust Buster has a high pH, offering a protective layer while effectively penetrating and loosening rust, suitable for mild to moderate corrosion. Neutral Rust Buster maintains a pH around 7, providing gentle rust removal without damaging underlying surfaces, suitable for delicate materials and routine maintenance.</p></p>

<p><p>The Helium Purifier market consists of two main types: Heated Helium Purifiers and Helium Cryogenic Purifiers. Heated Helium Purifiers operate at elevated temperatures to remove impurities, ensuring high-purity helium for various applications, including scientific and industrial uses. In contrast, Helium Cryogenic Purifiers utilize extremely low temperatures to condense and separate contaminants, achieving superior purity levels. Both types are essential in industries requiring ultra-pure helium, such as semiconductor manufacturing, MRI technology, and aerospace research, facilitating efficient helium extraction and utilization.</p></p>

<p><p>Automotive lane warning systems enhance driving safety by alerting or assisting drivers regarding lane positioning. The Lane Departure Warning (LDW) system detects unintentional lane drift and provides visual or auditory alerts. The Lane Keeping System (LKS) goes further by automatically steering the vehicle back into the lane when drifting occurs. Additionally, the "Other" category encompasses advanced technologies such as Lane Centring Assistance and road condition monitoring systems, which collectively contribute to safer driving experiences by minimizing lane-related accidents.</p></p>

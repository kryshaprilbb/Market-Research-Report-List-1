<p><ul><li>Single-axis</li><li>Two-axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light-duty gas turbine market can be segmented into single-axis and two-axis types based on their design and operational capabilities. Single-axis gas turbines are simpler, featuring a single rotating shaft that integrates the compressor and turbine, making them more compact and cost-effective, suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, two-axis gas turbines have a more complex design with separate shafts for the compressor and turbine, allowing for improved efficiency, higher power output, and greater flexibility in larger-scale operations.</p></p>

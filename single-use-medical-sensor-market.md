<p><p>The single-use medical sensor market encompasses various types, including optical dissolved oxygen sensors, pH sensors, and pressure sensors. Optical dissolved oxygen sensors are used for monitoring oxygen levels in bioprocesses, ensuring optimal conditions for cell growth. pH sensors measure acidity or alkalinity in fluids, crucial for maintaining desired chemical environments. Pressure sensors monitor fluid pressure in medical devices to ensure patient safety. Other sensors may include temperature or conductivity sensors, broadening the scope of single-use applications in healthcare settings.</p></p>

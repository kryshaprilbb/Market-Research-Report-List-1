<p><p>The arcade monitor market comprises three primary types: CRT, LCD, and LED monitors. CRT monitors, the classic choice, utilize cathode ray tubes, offering rich colors and deep blacks but are bulky and heavy. LCD monitors revolutionized the industry with slim profiles and energy efficiency, providing improved image quality and reduced eye strain. LED monitors, a further advancement, enhance LCD technology with better brightness and color accuracy while consuming less power. Each type caters to different gaming experiences and technological preferences.</p></p>

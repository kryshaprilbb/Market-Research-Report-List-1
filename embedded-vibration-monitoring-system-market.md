<p><p>The Embedded Vibration Monitoring System market includes various types of sensors and devices used for industrial machinery health assessment. Accelerometers measure vibration acceleration, capturing dynamic motion. Proximity probes monitor the distance between a sensor and a rotating shaft, detecting misalignment and wear. Velocity sensors gauge the speed of vibrations, providing insights into machine condition. Transmitters facilitate the remote transmission of data for real-time monitoring. Other innovations encompass advanced technologies and systems that enhance vibration analysis and predictive maintenance strategies.</p></p>

<p><p>The medical laser market encompasses four main types: </p><p>1. **Solid State Laser Systems** use a solid gain medium, offering high power and precision for surgical and cosmetic applications. </p><p>2. **Gas Laser Systems** utilize gas mixtures for cutting and coagulating tissue, known for their reliability and effectiveness in surgery. </p><p>3. **Dye Laser Systems** employ organic dyes as the gain medium, providing versatility in wavelengths for various medical applications. </p><p>4. **Diode Laser Systems** are compact, energy-efficient, and widely used in treatments like hair removal and dental procedures.</p></p>

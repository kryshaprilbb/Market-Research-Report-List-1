<p><p>The General Surgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) market includes three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single electrode for cutting and coagulation; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, which involve two tips for precision in coagulating tissue without affecting surrounding areas; and Vessel Sealing Units, specialized for sealing blood vessels using heat and pressure. These technologies enhance surgical precision, reduce bleeding, and promote faster recovery, driving their adoption in medical facilities worldwide.</p></p>

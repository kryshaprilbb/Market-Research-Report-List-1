<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Drive Module market comprises three essential components: motors, controllers, and transmissions. Motors are electric units that convert electrical energy into mechanical power, driving the vehicle. Controllers regulate the power flow between the battery and the motor, optimizing performance and efficiency. Transmissions manage the torque and speed, ensuring smooth acceleration and deceleration. Together, these components enable efficient energy usage and enhance the overall performance and driving experience of electric and hybrid vehicles in the emerging mobility landscape.</p></p>

<p><p>Tensiometers for surface tension measurement are categorized into static and dynamic types. Static tensiometers assess surface tension by measuring the force exerted on a solid, liquid, or gas interface at equilibrium. Common methods include the Wilhelmy plate and Du Noüy ring techniques. Dynamic tensiometers, on the other hand, evaluate the change in surface tension under rapid conditions, such as during liquids' movement or disturbance, using techniques like the drop volume method or maximum bubble pressure method. Each type serves specific applications across various industries.</p></p>

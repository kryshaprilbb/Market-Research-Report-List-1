<p><p>The Outsourced Semiconductor Testing Service market primarily includes wafer testing and integrated circuit (IC) testing. Wafer testing involves evaluating semiconductor wafers during the manufacturing process to identify defects and ensure quality, optimizing yield before packaging. IC testing focuses on assessing the performance and functionality of individual integrated circuits after packaging. Both services are crucial for semiconductor companies seeking to enhance product reliability while reducing in-house testing costs, enabling better resource allocation and faster time-to-market for their semiconductor products.</p></p>

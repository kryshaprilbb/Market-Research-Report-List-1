MDF is made from wood fibers bonded with resin, offering versatility and smooth surfaces for furniture and cabinetry. HDF, denser and more robust than MDF, is typically used in flooring and high-performance applications. Particleboard is constructed from wood chips and adhesive, serving as an economical option for furniture and interior fixtures. These products cater to diverse consumer needs in construction and interior design.</p></p>

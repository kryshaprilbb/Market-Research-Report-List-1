<p><p>The Industrial X-ray Testing Machines market is categorized into Hard X-ray and Soft X-ray machines. Hard X-ray machines utilize high-energy radiation for penetrating dense materials, making them ideal for inspecting heavy structures and metal components in industries like aerospace and automotive. Conversely, Soft X-ray machines produce lower-energy radiation, suitable for examining lighter materials, electronic components, and welds. Both types play crucial roles in non-destructive testing, ensuring material integrity and safety across various industrial applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The car park ventilation system market primarily consists of axial fans and jet fans, both essential for ensuring air quality and safety in enclosed parking structures. Axial fans are designed to move air parallel to the fan shaft, ideal for providing a constant airflow and diluting pollutants. Jet fans, on the other hand, create high-velocity air streams to effectively control smoke and heat during emergencies, promoting rapid evacuation. Together, these systems enhance overall ventilation efficiency and improve user comfort in car parks.</p></p>

<p><p>The Medium Radar System market comprises two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) Radars and Pulsed Radars. CW Radars emit a continuous signal, making them effective for speed detection and tracking moving objects, often used in traffic monitoring and automotive applications. Pulsed Radars, on the other hand, send short bursts of energy, providing higher resolution and range capabilities, suitable for air traffic control and weather monitoring. Both technologies play crucial roles in defense, surveillance, and various civilian applications.</p></p>

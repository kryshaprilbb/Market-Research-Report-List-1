<p><p>The pneumatic cylinders market can be segmented into three main types: single-acting cylinders, double-acting cylinders, and others. Single-acting cylinders utilize air pressure to extend the piston while a spring returns it, making them suitable for simple applications. Double-acting cylinders use air pressure for both extending and retracting, offering greater force and control, ideal for more complex tasks. Other types may include rotary or special-purpose cylinders, catering to specific industrial needs and enhancing versatility in automation processes.</p></p>

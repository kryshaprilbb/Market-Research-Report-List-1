<p><p>The fire retardant fabric market consists of two main types: inherent fire retardant fabric and treated fire retardant fabric. Inherent fire retardant fabric is made from fibers that possess fire-resistant properties inherently, providing long-lasting protection without chemical treatments. Treated fire retardant fabric, on the other hand, involves the application of chemical finishes or treatments to regular fabrics to enhance their fire resistance. Both types are essential for various applications, including industrial, military, and home textiles, ensuring safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>

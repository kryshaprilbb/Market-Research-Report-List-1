Monolayer films consist of a single layer of graphite, offering excellent thermal management in compact applications. Multiple layer films enhance thermal conductivity further, ideal for applications requiring superior heat dissipation. Composite films combine graphite with other materials, improving flexibility and mechanical strength while maintaining thermal performance.

<p><ul><li>Capacitive Voltage Dividers</li><li>Compensated Resistive Voltage Dividers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high voltage divider market is primarily categorized into capacitive voltage dividers and compensated resistive voltage dividers. Capacitive voltage dividers utilize capacitors to divide voltage, offering high accuracy and a wide frequency range, making them ideal for measuring high voltages in varying applications. Compensated resistive voltage dividers, on the other hand, use resistors adjusted for temperature variations, ensuring stable performance under different conditions. Both types are essential for precise voltage measurements in industrial, power generation, and testing environments.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Water Coolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Coolers</li><li>Plumbed-in Water Coolers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water cooler market is primarily divided into two types: bottled water coolers and plumbed-in water coolers. Bottled water coolers utilize large water bottles for dispensing, making them portable and easy to install but requiring regular bottle replacement. In contrast, plumbed-in water coolers are connected directly to a water supply, providing a continuous flow of filtered water, which eliminates the need for bottle replacements but involves a more complex installation process. Both types cater to various consumer needs for hydration.</p></p>

In contrast, headset type devices encompass the entire head, often resembling traditional headphones, delivering a broader area of stimulation. Both types aim to aid in the treatment of anxiety, depression, and insomnia by influencing brain activity through electrical impulses, catering to various user preferences.</p></p>

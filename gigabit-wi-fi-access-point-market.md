<p><p>The Gigabit Wi-Fi Access Point market comprises two primary types: Wireless Access Points (WAPs) and Wireless LAN Controllers (WLCs). Wireless Access Points facilitate connectivity for devices in a network, enabling high-speed internet access and often supporting multiple users simultaneously. Wireless LAN Controllers manage multiple WAPs to optimize performance, security, and configuration, ensuring seamless connectivity across a larger area. Together, they enhance network efficiency, scalability, and user experience in various environments, including enterprises, schools, and public spaces.</p></p>

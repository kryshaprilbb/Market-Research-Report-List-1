<p><strong>The Ground Mounting Solar Panel Tracker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Dual Axis Solar Trackers</li><li>Active Dual Axis Solar Trackers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ground mounting solar panel tracker market is primarily categorized into passive and active dual-axis solar trackers. Passive dual-axis solar trackers utilize natural elements, such as thermal expansion or gravitational forces, to adjust the panels’ orientation, ensuring maximum sunlight exposure without external power. In contrast, active dual-axis solar trackers employ motors and sensors to dynamically adjust the panel position based on real-time solar movement, optimizing energy capture throughout the day. Both types enhance solar efficiency, catering to varying installation needs and budgets.</p></p>

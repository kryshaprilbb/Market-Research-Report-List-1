<p><strong>The Commercial Sous Vide Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immersion Types</li><li>Water Bath Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial sous vide machines are categorized mainly into two types: immersion and water bath. Immersion circulators are compact devices that heat water and circulate it, allowing chefs to cook food in vacuum-sealed bags. They are portable and ideal for small kitchens. Water bath types are larger, often built-in units that maintain a consistent temperature in a larger water reservoir, suitable for high-volume cooking. Both types provide precise temperature control for consistent and high-quality results in food preparation.</p></p>

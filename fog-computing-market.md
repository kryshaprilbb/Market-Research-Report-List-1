<p><p>Fog computing is a decentralized computing infrastructure that extends cloud capabilities closer to the edge of the network. It enables data processing, storage, and analysis at or near the source of data generation, resulting in reduced latency, improved response times, and enhanced bandwidth efficiency. This paradigm is particularly beneficial for applications requiring real-time analytics, such as IoT, autonomous vehicles, and smart cities.</p><p>The Fog Computing Market is poised for substantial growth, driven by increasing demand for real-time data processing and the proliferation of IoT devices. Key sectors such as smart transportation, healthcare, and manufacturing are increasingly adopting fog computing solutions to enhance operational efficiency and decision-making processes. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.1% during the forecast period, reflecting rising investment in edge infrastructure and advancements in AI and machine learning technologies.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift towards greater integration of fog computing with other technologies, such as 5G, which facilitates faster data transmission. Furthermore, the growing emphasis on data privacy and security has led businesses to adopt fog computing as a means to localize sensitive data processing. As industries evolve, fog computing will play a crucial role in shaping the future of distributed computing architectures.</p></p>

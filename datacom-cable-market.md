<p><p>The Datacom Cable Market is primarily divided into two types: copper cables and fiber optic cables. Copper cables, such as twisted pair and coaxial cables, are widely used for short-distance communication due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. In contrast, fiber optic cables utilize glass fibers to transmit data as light signals, offering higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances, making them ideal for high-speed internet and telecommunication applications. Both types cater to various networking needs in different industries.</p></p>
